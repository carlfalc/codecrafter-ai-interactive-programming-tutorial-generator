Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from existing coding platforms? +
Unlike static platforms like Udemy or Coursera, CodeCrafter AI generates personalized tutorials on-demand based on your specific project goals. Instead of following pre-made courses, you describe what you want to build and our AI creates a custom learning path that adapts to your skill level in real-time.
What programming languages and frameworks are supported? +
Our AI supports all major programming languages including JavaScript, Python, Java, C#, Go, Rust, and more. We cover popular frameworks like React, Vue, Angular, Django, Flask, Spring, Node.js, and can instantly create content for new technologies as they emerge.
How does the AI adapt to my skill level? +
Our AI monitors your progress through coding challenges and adjusts difficulty in real-time. If you're struggling, it provides more scaffolding and simpler challenges. If you're excelling, it introduces more complex concepts and edge cases. The system learns your learning patterns to optimize your educational journey.
Can I use this for team training? +
Yes! Our Team and Enterprise plans include collaboration features, progress tracking for managers, and the ability to create custom learning paths for your organization's specific tech stack and project requirements.
What if I get stuck on a challenge? +
Our AI mentor uses Socratic questioning to guide you toward solutions without giving them away. You'll get contextual hints, relevant documentation links, and questions that help you think through the problem. This develops genuine problem-solving skills rather than copy-paste habits.
